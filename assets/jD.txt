Data Science / Computer Vision Intern


About Khel.AI

DRS by Khel.AI was born out of the founders’ passion for cricket and the vision to

revolutionise the sport using advanced AI technologies. We are a team of cricket enthusiasts

and tech innovators building cutting-edge solutions that make the game fairer, more

engaging, and more accessible.

Our work spans computer vision, machine learning, sensor fusion, and real-time sports

analytics — all centred around the modern cricket ecosystem.


Role Overview

As a Data Science / Computer Vision Intern at Khel.AI, you will contribute to building

next-generation cricket-tech products. You will work on real match data, multi-camera

setups, and innovative AI solutions that directly impact how the sport is analysed and

experienced.


Key Responsibilities

● Develop and enhance technologies involving cameras, sensors, and computer

vision to improve accuracy and system performance.

● Apply machine learning and data analysis techniques to refine existing models and

extract actionable insights.

● Collaborate with cross-functional engineering teams to design and implement new

product features.

● Research and experiment with the latest advancements in CV, ML, and sports

analytics.

● Contribute to building modules such as ball tracking, player tracking,

multi-camera calibration, scoring systems, and performance analysis.


Required Skills & Qualifications

● Strong problem-solving abilities and an analytical mindset.

● Proven project experience in software development, machine learning, or computer

● Hands-on experience with Python and related ML/CV workflows.

● Passion for sports — especially cricket — and excitement to work on real-world

Desirable Skills

● Strong communication skills to explain technical concepts to diverse teams.
